Peugeot Isen u'Select Salt & Pepper Mill Set

Elevate your cooking with the precision and freshness of this French-made salt and pepper mill set. Designed for everyday use, it combines elegance with functionality to bring out the best in your favorite spices.

French Crafted for Quality
Made in France, this set is a testament to Peugeot's commitment to exceptional craftsmanship and timeless design. Each component is built to ensure durability and a refined kitchen experience.

Stainless Steel Spice Scoop
The included stainless steel spice scoop is both sturdy and easy to clean, making it perfect for professional and casual use. It helps you measure and grind your spices with ease, adding convenience to your cooking routine.

Patented u'Select Grind System
This innovative U'Select system allows for 6 different grind settings, so you can adjust the coarseness of your salt and pepper to suit any dish. Whether you're adding a fine sprinkle to a salad or a coarse grind to a steak, the results are unmatched.

Beechwood Construction with Natural Finish
The beechwood body is not only stylish but also enhances the aroma and flavor of your spices when ground. Its natural finish adds a warm, organic feel to your kitchen while ensuring a smooth and consistent grind every time.

Eco-Friendly & French Sourced
Sourced from France, this mill is made with sustainable materials and processes, reflecting Peugeot's dedication to environmental responsibility without compromising on quality or performance.

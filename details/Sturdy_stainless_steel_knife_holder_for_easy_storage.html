ZAEGO Stainless Steel Knife Holder

Keep your kitchen knives organized and protected with this durable, space-saving knife holder. Crafted from high-quality stainless steel, it ensures strength and reliability for everyday use.

Sturdy & Durable Construction

Our knife holder is built to last with a robust stainless steel frame that resists corrosion, rust, and wear. It's the perfect addition to any kitchen or home setting, offering a reliable storage solution that keeps your blades safe and ready.

Space-Saving & Efficient Design

Featuring 6 slots, this compact holder allows you to store and access your slicing and chopping knives quickly and easily. Its streamlined shape means it takes up less space without sacrificing functionality, making it ideal for small kitchens.

The included non-slip EVA pads ensure your knives are held firmly in place, preventing any accidental movement. This not only keeps your countertop clean and protected but also adds a layer of safety during handling and storage.

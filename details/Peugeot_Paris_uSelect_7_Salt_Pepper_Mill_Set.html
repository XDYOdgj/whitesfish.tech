Peugeot Paris u'Select Salt & Pepper Mill Set

Enhance your cooking experience with our elegant and functional Peugeot Paris u'Select Salt & Pepper Mill Set. Designed to deliver the perfect balance of flavor to every dish, these mills combine precision, style, and user-friendly features to make your kitchen more efficient and enjoyable.

The mocha-colored salt mill features a fine, clean grind that ensures even seasoning, while the ash-colored pepper mill provides a bold, rich flavor profile. Whether you're in the kitchen or at the dining table, these mills are the ideal addition to any fine dining setup or home cooking space.

Crafted from sustainable PEFC-certified beechwood, this set offers both durability and an eco-conscious appeal. The sleek, high-quality finish adds a touch of sophistication to your table setting, while the stainless steel components ensure long-lasting performance and easy cleaning.

Each mill in the set is pre-filled with premium rock salt and peppercorns, ready to elevate your meals from the moment you open the box. The included refilling cap makes restocking simple, so you can always keep your seasoning fresh and flavorful.
